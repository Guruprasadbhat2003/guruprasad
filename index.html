Traveling Through Time: From Ancient Civilizations to Medieval Adventures

As we got ready for our next assignments, we wanted to explore history in a fun and exciting way. We decided to look at how things changed from a long time ago to the Middle Ages. Together—Dharani, Irfan, Hrishikesh, Radhi.—we chose two big topics to focus on: the Chola Empire in South Asia and the Crusades around the world.

We started by doing research, which was like going on a treasure hunt for information. We read books, checked out websites, and watched videos to learn as much as we could about the Chola Empire and the Crusades. Each new thing we discovered felt like finding a hidden treasure, and it made us even more excited to share what we learned with our classmates.

Once we had all our facts, we wanted to come up with a fun way to present them. That’s when we got the idea for a game show! Instead of just talking at our classmates, we wanted to get them involved and make learning about history feel like a game.

So, here’s how it worked: teams would answer questions about the Chola Empire and the Crusades, and for every question they got right, they’d earn points. But we wanted to make it even more exciting, so we added a challenge. Teams had to build a pyramid out of paper cups,and the first team to finish would get a chance to answer a question with a little hint to help them out.

Now, here’s the fun part: if a team got a question wrong, they’d have to do something silly as a punishment. It could be anything from singing a funny song to doing a little dance. We thought it would make the game even more hilarious and keep everyone laughing.

As we finish up, learning about the Chola Empire and the Crusades was really fun. We worked together and had a great time exploring history. Now, we’re excited for more adventures in learning!
